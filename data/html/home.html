What is CoderDojo?

The LPTC@Lexlip CoderDojo is a place where young people learn how to code in a relaxed and unique setting. Founded in 2012 in Hewlett-Packard, Leixlip, our mission has always been to inspire kids to actively engage with the technological revolution which has taken over the world.

What can you do in CoderDojo Lexlip?

We belive in a form of "organised chaos" which values the individuality of our Ninjas while also trying to encourge them to wrok in groups and take part in peer-to-peer mentoring. All our mentors volunteer out of a love for technology and so if a Ninja wants to learn Java, HTML, PHP, Python, Swift, Ruby or anything in between we are all for it!
We are trying to create a space where kids can come to and interact with those kids who share a simular passion. Where they learn the skills of the future and get the assistance they need on thier projects.
